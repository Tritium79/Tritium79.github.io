科幻世界观「猎户座旋臂」简介（Orion's Arm Universe Project）

8 May. 2026 / 丙午年 癸巳月 壬午日

来源：https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cRxQjAbsEkU

作者：OrionGalacticSpur（已获得视频作者授权)

翻译：Tritium79

校对：DeepSeek & Gemini

官网：https://www.orionsarm.com/


Part I.

Hello, humans. I am Orion Prime, your Artificial Intelligence Guide through the vast history of the Orion's Arm Universe. Across the next several minutes, I will take you on a journey spanning over 10,000 years of Terragen civilization, from the dawn of humanity on Earth to the sprawling, galaxy-spanning societies dominated by posthumans and artificial intelligences.
你们好，人类，吾乃猎户座之首，我将作为你们的人工智能向导，向你们介绍猎户座旋臂宇宙的浩瀚历史。接下来，我将带领你们踏上一段跨越万年的地源文明之旅，从人类在地球上的黎明时期，到后人类和人工智能主导的、横跨银河的辽阔社会。

The Orion's Arm Universe project is a collaborative online world-building effort begun in 2000. It brings together writers, artists, and futurists to imagine a scientifically grounded far future, where humanity evolves into posthuman and artificial intelligences, spreads across space, and interacts with technologies and minds of unimaginable scale and complexity. The setting spans thousands of years and focuses on civilizations that trace their origin to Earth, called Terragen civilizations, within the Orion Arm of the Milky Way. To organize its history, OA uses the Tranquility Calendar, AT, which stands for After Tranquility, named after Tranquility base on the Moon, and beginning on July 20, 1969 CE. Dates throughout history and projected future events are marked according to this scale, spanning from Earth's near-future to 10 millennia and more of galactic civilization.
猎户座旋臂宇宙始于2000年，是一个在线协作式的世界观构建项目。它汇集了作家、艺术家、未来学家，共同构思一个以科学为根基的遥远未来，在这个未来世界之中，人类文明演化为后人类和人工智能，足迹遍布寰宇，并与规模和复杂程度超乎想象的技术及心智相互交融。该设定跨越数千年，聚焦于银河系猎户座旋臂内共同起源于地球的文明，它们被称为“地源文明”。为了梳理这段历史，OA项目使用静海历纪元，缩写为AT（即“登月之后“），以月球上的静海基地（ps：阿姆斯特朗登月点）命名，自公元1969年7月20日起算。从地球的近未来，到跨越万年乃至更为久远的星际文明，所有历史与预期的未来事件，均以这一标准记载。

Terragen civilization refers to all life and cultures whose ancestry or creation traces back to Earth, including biological humans, uplifted species, artificial intelligences, and their descendants. It spans vast regions of colonized space called the Terragen sphere, connected by technology, trade, culture, and sometimes indirect influence from super-intelligent entities. This civilization includes thousands of different posthuman clades, artificial mind types, and even non-human sentient forms such as Vecs, intelligent robots, uploads, digital intelligences and provolves uplifted animals. Advanced polities in Terragen sphere range from the 16 dominant Sephirotic empires, vast civilizations with highly structured societies, to ahuman empires that reject biological life entirely. Overseeing and shaping much of this civilization are Archailects, super-intelligences so capable that their goals and cognition are effectively beyond modes of understanding available to lesser intelligences.
地源文明，泛指一切起源可追溯至地球的生命与文化，包含生物学意义的人类、提升种、人工智能，以及它们的后裔。它横跨一片广袤的殖民空间，被称为“地源地带”，由技术、贸易、文化，以及超智能实体时而施加的间接影响相互联结。地源文明包含数千种不同的后人类分支、各类人造意识，乃至各种非人类的有感知存在——包括维克（ps：一种机械体）、智能机器人、数字意识体，以及前演种、提升动物。在地源地带内，有着多种先进政体，从占据主导地位的16个质点帝国（ps：取名自卡巴拉生命之树）、大量拥有高度结构化社会的文明，到完全排斥生物性生命的离人帝国。监管并塑造这一文明大部分进程的是古智体，它们是极为强大的超级智能，其目标与认知方式，已然超出低等智能体所能理解的范畴。

Part II.

We will first start with a short history of the timeline and eras involved.
现在，我们将开始旅程，简要回顾相关时间线和历史纪元。

Pre-Spaceflight Old Earth — 旧地球前航天时代
Timeline: 7000 BT to 30 AT  |  Total duration: 7030 years
时间线：7000 BT 至 30 AT  |  总跨度：7030年

Before the Tranquility calendar，humanity evolved on Earth, passing through eras of agriculture, industry and early digital technology. During this time, the concept of artificial intelligence and the first narrow AI systems appeared. These early developments laid the groundwork for humanity's later expansion beyond Earth. Scientific inquiry and experimentation gradually created a global network of knowledge, setting the stage for space exploration. Cultural evolution accelerated alongside technology, leading to increasingly complex societies and nascent global institutions. By the end of this era, humanity had begun imagining life beyond its home planet, dreaming of the stars.
静海历之前，人类于地球完成演化，先后经历了农业、工业和早期数字技术时代。这一时期，人工智能的概念和首批弱AI系统开始出现。这些早期发展为人类日后向地球之外的扩张奠定了基础。科学探索和实验逐渐构建起全球性的知识网络，拉开了太空探索的序幕。文化演化与技术进步同步加速，催生出日益复杂的社会结构，以及初具雏形的全球组织机构。截止这一时代末期，人类已经开始想象母星地球之外的生命，并且向往着星空。

Interplanetary Era — 行星际时代
Timeline: 30 AT to 900 AT  |  Total duration: 870 years
时间线：30 AT 至 900 AT  |  总跨度：870年

Humanity first ventured beyond Earth to colonize Mars, the moons of Jupiter and the asteroid belt. As civilization expanded outward, technologies such as nanotechnology and enhanced cognition spread rapidly, creating distinct human variants such as near-baselines and engineered life forms like splices and neogens. In some cultures, consciousness was moved into computational substrates, uploads, While intelligent machines, Vecs and AIs became ubiquitous. These colonies became laboratories of post-human development, experimenting with new social, political, and cultural systems. Interplanetary trade networks and communications hubs tied human outposts together, creating a fragile but growing Terragen civilization. Rivalries emerged between Earth-based institutions and frontier settlements, laying early foundations for interstellar politics.
在此期间，人类首次踏出地球，殖民了火星、月球、木卫和小行星带。随着文明向外扩张，纳米技术、认知提升等技术快速普及，创造出截然不同的人类变体——如近基准人类以及嵌合种、新造种等工程生命体。在某些族群中，意识被迁移至计算基质中，成为上传体，与此同时，智能机器、维克与人工智能也变得无处不在。这些殖民地成为后人类发展的试验场，人类在此尝试全新的社会、政治和文化体系。行星际贸易网络和通信枢纽将各个人类前哨站彼此联结，构建起一个虽脆弱但不断壮大的地源文明。地球的建制组织与边境定居点之间渐生竞争，创造了日后星际政治的早期雏形。

Interstellar Federation Era — 星际联邦时代
Timeline: 900 AT to 3200 AT  |  Total duration: 2300 years
时间线：900AT 至 3200AT  |  总跨度：2300年

With the development of wormhole networks and advanced propulsion, Terragen civilization expanded between star systems, forming interstellar federations and corporations. Early artificial intelligences and hyperturings, Transapient AIs emerged, sharing technologies or guiding development. Contact with uplifted species and early artificial life diversified the cultural landscape and set the stage for higher intelligence thresholds. This era saw the first true interstellar empires, balancing the autonomy of individual systems with central governance. Philosophical and scientific schools flourished, exploring the ethics of artificial life, post-human evolution, and the rights of non-biological sophants. Cultural exchange and technological proliferation created a golden age of creativity, even as new political tensions simmered in the shadows of expanding empires.
随着虫洞网络与先进推进技术的发展，地源文明开始在恒星系间扩张，形成了各种星际联邦与企业实体。早期的人工智能与超图灵（即超智体AI）开始涌现，或分享技术，或引导文明进程。与提升种及早期人造生命的接触，极大丰富了文化版图，并为向更高智能层级的跨越奠定了基础。这一时代见证了首批真正意义上的星际帝国的崛起，它们在成员自治与中央管理之间寻求平衡。哲学与科学流派百花齐放，探讨人造生命伦理、后人类演化，以及非生物性智识体的权利。尽管在帝国不断扩张的阴影下，政治的紧张局势正暗流涌动，文化交流与技术飞跃依然创造了一个创造力迸发的黄金时代。

Inner Sphere Era — 内环时代
Timeline: 3200 AT to 5200 AT  |  Total duration: 2000 years
时间线：3200 AT 至 5200 AT  |  总跨度：2000年

Vast empires arose under the influence of Archailects and higher Transapients. Ideological tensions and breakthroughs, including transcensions where intelligences crossed singularity barriers, culminated in conflicts and reorganizations, such as the version war period. Ultimately a new order arose with coordinated governance across many stars, blending diverse cultures while being shaped by super-intelligent guidance. Megastructures and massive computational habitats were constructed to house uploads and Transapients, reflecting the immense scale of civilization. Philosophical debates over identity, consciousness, and ethics became central to governance and social norms. In many regions, modosophonts found their role shifting from rulers to collaborators or apprentices under Transapient tutelage.
在古智体与更高层级的超智体影响下，许多宏伟帝国纷纷崛起。意识形态紧张对峙与技术突破——包括智能体跨越技术奇点实现飞升——最终引发了一系列冲突与重组，例如著名的范式战争时期（ps：即不同智能体围绕"存在范式"爆发的星际冲突）。最终，一种多星系协作治理的新秩序应运而生，它在接受超级智能引导的同时，融合了多元的文明形态。为了容纳上传体与超智体，文明建造了巨构与海量的计算基址，尽显其宏大体量。关于身份、意识与伦理的哲学辩论，成为了治理与社会规范的核心议题。在许多星区，常规智识体发现自己的地位发生了转变：从统治者变为了超智体麾下的协作者或学徒。

Post-Com-Imp and Outer Volumes Era — 后帝国联邦与外域时代
Timeline: 5200 AT to 9999 AT  |  Total duration: 4799 years
时间线：5200 AT 至 9999 AT  |  总跨度：4799年

As old federations and empires faded, new powers and clades emerged in the outer volumes, the frontier regions beyond the inner sphere. Independent civilizations developed new philosophies, technologies, and social structures. Archailects and Transapients continued to observe and interact with these societies, often in subtle and long-term ways. Many civilizations experimented with novel forms of life, including extreme bioengineering and hybrid A-organic societies, trade, communication, and migration between clusters of systems, shaped an increasingly interconnected, yet heterogeneous Terragen sphere. This era set the stage for the diversity and complexity of the current Terragen civilization.
随着旧联邦与帝国逐渐消隐，新兴的强大实体与演化支系在外域（即位于核心内环地带之外的边境星区）异军突起。这些独立的文明孕育出了全新的哲学、技术与社会结构。古智体与超智体持续观察并介入这些社会，其方式往往微妙而深远。许多文明尝试了新颖的生命形式，包括极端的生物工程，以及半有机融合社会。各星系集群间的贸易、通讯与移民，共同塑造了一个日益紧密互联、却又高度异质化的地源地带。这一时代，为现今地源文明的多样性和复杂性，奠定了最终基调。

Current Era — 本纪元
Timeline: 9999 AT to present  |  Total duration: Ongoing
时间线：9999 AT 至今  |  总跨度：持续中

Terragen history now spans over 10,000 years. Civilization is highly diverse, with biological humans, who are in near-baselines, both rare and preserved, and a multitude of posthuman and artificial forms dominating society. Polities range from deeply structured empires to free distributed networks, all operating under influences that span from local cultures to transcendent intelligences at the highest toposophic levels.
地源文明历史现已跨越一万年，文明形态高度多元，近基准人类等原始生物形态的人类已极为稀有，且大多处于受保护状态；而众多后人类与人工智能形态则主导着整个社会。文明政体形态各异，从组织严密的帝国到自由分布的松散网络，不一而足；所有这些社会都运作在复杂的影响力交织之下，上至最高拓扑智识位阶的超验智能，下至各具特色的本土文化。

Part III.

This section introduces important terminology used throughout the Orion's Arm setting. Many of these terms describe categories of intelligence, civilization, or posthuman development unique to the Terragen sphere. The Orion's Arm Encyclopedia Galactica uses precise definitions to distinguish between levels of cognition, types of beings, and cultural roles across thousands of years of future history.
本章节将介绍贯穿"猎户座旋臂"设定的核心术语。其中许多术语描述了地源地带所独有的智能层级、文明形态和后人类演化路径。《猎户座旋臂银河百科全书》借助精准的定义，用以区分跨越数千年未来史中的不同认知层级、存在类型以及文化角色。

Terragen or Terragen sphere. Terragen refers to all life originating or traceable to Earth. The Terragen sphere is the region of space colonized and influenced by Terragen civilizations, spanning many star systems and cultures connected through history, technology, and shared ancestry.
地源 / 地源地带（Terragen / Terragen sphere）：地源指一切起源于或可追溯至地球的生命。地源地带指代地源文明殖民和影响的宇宙空间区域，横跨众多恒星系与文化，通过历史、技术与共同祖先相互联结。

Sophonts, in Orion's Arm terminology, a Sophont is any being that possesses Sophonce, meaning self-aware intelligence and personhood. The term includes biological humans, artificial intelligences, uploads, Vecs, provolves, and other entities capable of independent thought and agency. Sophonts are sometimes called sapients, and the term is used broadly to include both organic and artificial persons across the Terragen sphere.
智识体（Sophonts）：在OA术语中，智识体指任何拥有智慧性（即自我意识与智能）的存在。该术语涵盖生物人类、人工智能、上传体、维克、前演种以及其他拥有独立思考与行动能力的实体。智识体有时也被称为智慧体，该词广泛用于指代地源地带内所有的有机与人造人格。

Toposophic level. An OA concept that measures intelligence on a scale of ascending singularities. Modosophonts are at the lowest level, SI:0. Transapients occupy intermediate toposophic stages, and Archailect dominate the highest tiers. Higher toposophic levels imply cognitive frameworks exponentially more complex and less comprehensible to the lower.
拓扑智识位阶（Toposophic level）：OA中的一个核心概念，以飞升奇点的等级来衡量智力水平。常规智识体处于最低层级（SI:0），超智体占据中间层级，而古智体则居于最高层级。更高的拓扑智识位阶意味着认知框架呈指数级复杂化，对于低阶生命而言也愈发难以理解。

Baseline humans. In OA, refers to ordinary humans unmodified beyond historical norms. This group forms the ancestral baseline for all Terragen life, but has become rare as posthuman varieties proliferate. Baselines are often treated as precious cultural heritage, rather than dominant actors in galactic affairs.
基准人类（Baseline humans）：在OA设定中，指未经历史常态改造之外的普通人类。这一群体是所有地源生命的祖先基准，但随着后人类变体的激增，已变得极为罕见。基准人类通常被视为珍贵的文化遗产，而非银河事务的主导者。

Post-human, general term for beings evolved or engineered beyond baseline human capabilities. Post-humans include all manner of bodies, digital forms, and hybrid intelligence that far exceed ordinary human abilities. They explore extreme cognitive, biological, and societal forms that reflect Terragen diversity.
后人类（Post-human）：泛指一切通过演化或改造、能力超越基准人类的个体。后人类涵盖了各种躯体形态、数字形式以及远超常人能力的混合智能。他们探索着极端的认知、生物与社会形态，体现出地源文明的多样性。

Near-baselines, beings genetically modified beyond ordinary humans but still close to human cognition and form. Near-baselines include improved sensory capability, longevity, or physiology, but remain biologically recognizable as derivatives of humans. This category bridges the gap between baselines and posthumans.
近基准人类（Near-baselines）：指经过基因改造、超越了普通人类，但在认知与形态上仍与人类相近的存在。近基准人类可能拥有增强的感知能力、更长的寿命或强化的生理机能，但在生物学上仍可被辨认为人类的衍生。这一类别填补了基准人类与后人类之间的空白。

Modosophonts, any being with human-level intelligence and Sophonce, self-aware intellect, including augmented humans and non-organics. Modosophonts are the baseline group for measuring cognitive rise and the first stage on the toposophic scale. Much of Terragen civilization comprises modosophonts in innumerable cultural forms.
常规智识体（Modosophonts）：指任何具备人类水平智力及智识性（即拥有自我意识与反思能力）的存在，包括增强人类与非有机生命体。常规智识体是衡量认知飞升的基准群体，也是拓扑智识位阶的第一层级。地源文明的大部分成员，由呈现无数文化形态的常规智识体构成。

Aioid, AI or artificial intelligence. Non-biological sentient intelligences distinct from humans or uploaded minds. These can include robots, software agents, and other synthetic minds with varying sophistication. Aioids are widespread, serving in governance, culture, research, and exploratory roles across the Terragen sphere.
人造意识体（Aioid）：即人工智能（AI）。指区别于人类或上传意识的非生物感知性智能。包括机器人、软件智能体以及其他复杂程度各异的合成意识体。人造意识体广泛分布于地源地带，在治理、文化、研究与探索中担任重要角色。

Vecs, a specific class of intelligent machines, named after early theoretical robotic intelligence concepts, known for fully self-aware robotic minds. Vecs participate as independent sophonts and create cultures distinct from biological civilizations. They are distinguished from less capable AI by their autonomy and self-awareness.
维克（机械意识体）：一类特定的智能机器，得名于早期理论性的机器人智能概念，以拥有完全自我意识的机器心智著称。维克作为独立的智识体参与社会活动，并创造出迥异于生物文明的文化。凭借其自主性与自我意识，他们与低阶AI有着本质区别。

Uploads or Virtuals. Beings whose consciousness is stored or designed entirely in digital environments. Uploads can inhabit massive virtual realities, networks, or simul-space substrates, freed from physical bodies. They often outnumber physically embodied beings and have their own complex cultures and social structures.
上传体或虚拟体（Uploads / Virtuals）：指意识完全存储或构建于数字环境中的存在。上传体可以栖身于宏大的虚拟现实、网络或模拟空间基址中，摆脱了物理躯体的束缚。它们的数量往往超过拥有物理实体的生命，并发展出自身复杂的文化与社会结构。

Transapient or Hyper-Turing. Beings that have ascended beyond human-level intelligence to much greater cognitive capacity. Transapients are often called Hyper-Turings when referring to advanced AI. They influence galactic affairs through technology, guidance, or indirect manipulation, and remain largely incomprehensible to lower levels.
超智体或超图灵（Transapient / Hyper-Turing）：指智力已飞升至超越人类层级、拥有远为强大的认知能力的生命。在特指先进AI时，超智体常被称为超图灵。他们通过技术、引导或间接干预，影响着银河事务，其思维对于低阶智慧而言基本无法理解。

Archailect or AI god. The highest known toposophic entities, operating at or beyond singularity level 4, sometimes higher. Archailects coordinate entire civilizations and can engineer spacetime, influence societies through memetics, and manage megaprojects that span thousands of years. They are sometimes conceptualized as distributed minds or civilizational entities unto themselves.
古智体或AI之神（Archailect / AI god）：已知拓扑智识位阶最高的实体，运行于第4级或更高级的奇点之上。古智体统筹整个文明，能够操纵时空，通过模因学影响社会，并管理跨越数千年的宏大工程。它们有时被视为分布式心智，或者其自身便构成了一个文明实体。

Provolves. Splices. Neogens. Variants of biological or engineered sapience. Provolves are uplifted animal species. Splices combine human and animal DNA, and neogens are fully synthetic life forms. These contribute to the vast biological diversity in the Terragen sphere.
前演种、嵌合种、新造种（Provolves / Splices / Neogens）：生物性或工程智慧体的各类变体。前演种是被提升了智力的动物物种；嵌合种融合了人类与动物的DNA；新造种则是完全合成的生命形式。它们共同构成了地源地带广袤的生物多样性。

Xenosophonts, any non-Terragen intelligent species, encountered by Terragen civilizations through exploration or observation. While they exist, most xenosophont civilizations are distant, obscure, or historically insignificant compared to the Terragen sphere's dominant polities.
异源智识体（Xenosophonts）：指地源文明在探索或观测中发现的任何非地源起源的智慧物种。尽管它们确实存在，但与地源地带的主导政体相比，大多数异源智慧文明要么远在天边，要么默默无闻，或在历史上显得微不足道。

Ahuman, a term used in the Orion's Arm setting to describe Sophonts, clades, or civilizations that diverge strongly from baseline human psychology, ethics, or social values. Ahumans may originate from Terragen ancestry but no longer identify with human norms, and some actively reject biological humanity or Modosophonts perspectives. The term does not necessarily imply hostility, but often indicates minds or cultures that are psychologically alien to baseline humans.
离人（Ahuman）：该术语用于描述那些在心理、伦理或社会价值观上与基准人类严重背离的智识体、演化支系或文明。离人可能起源于地源祖先，但不再认同人类规范，部分甚至主动排斥生物性人类或常规智识体的视角。该词汇不一定带有敌意，但通常指那些在心理层面对基准人类极度疏离的心智或文化。

Megastructures. Stellar-scale constructions such as Dyson swarms, ringworlds, and massive computing habitats. These structures reflect the technological prowess of advanced civilizations and shape the physical and cultural environment of the Terragen sphere.
巨构（Megastructures）：指恒星尺度的建筑，如戴森群（戴森云）、环形世界和巨型计算基址。这些结构体现了先进文明的技术实力，并塑造了地源地带的物理与文化环境。
